### 1. Descriptive Analytics & Traditional Leaderboards

You can easily slice the dataset to find the standard league leaders, but you can go deeper by looking at efficiency rather than just volume:

* **The Clinical Finishers:** Look beyond total `Goals`. Rank players by `Conversion %` or `Shots On Target` relative to total `Shots` to find out who is the most lethal in front of goal.
* **The Creative Engines:** Combine `Assists`, `Through Balls`, `fThird Passes`, and columns like `Carries Ended with Chance` to evaluate who the true playmakers are.
* **Defensive Rocks:** Rank defenders by their volume and success rate using `Tackles`, `Interceptions`, `gDuels %` (Ground Duels), and `aDuels %` (Aerial Duels).
* **The Golden Glove Analysis:** Rank goalkeepers by `Clean Sheets`, `Saves %`, and the advanced stat **`Goals Prevented`** (which measures shot-stopping quality against expectation).

### 2. "Per 90 Minute" Normalization (Fair Comparisons)

Raw volumes bias results toward players who play every single minute. You can normalize key metrics per 90 minutes played using the formula:

$$\text{Metric Per 90} = \frac{\text{Metric}}{\text{Minutes}} \times 90$$

* This allows you to find **"hidden gems"**—impact substitutes or rotation players who produce high numbers (e.g., Goals per 90, Tackles per 90, Chanced Created per 90) despite fewer minutes on the pitch.

### 3. Feature Engineering & Technical Performance Metrics

The columns containing percentage values (`Conversion %`, `Passes%`, `Crosses %`, `fThird Passes %`, `gDuels %`, `aDuels %`, `Saves %`) are currently stored as strings due to the `%` sign. You can clean these columns to turn them into numeric values and calculate:

* **Passing Progressiveness vs. Accuracy:** Compare `Passes%` with `fThird Passes %`. A midfielder might have a lower overall pass accuracy because they constantly attempt high-risk, high-reward forward passes.
* **Ball Carrier Efficiency:** Look at the ratio of `Progressive Carries` to `Carries Ended with Shot` or `Carries Ended with Goal` to see which dribblers convert their runs into tangible team threats.
* **Discipline & Aggression Index:** Combine `Fouls`, `Yellow Cards`, and `Red Cards` per 90 minutes to rank the most aggressive or error-prone players in the league.

### 4. Correlation & Relationship Visualizations

Using scatter plots and trendlines, you can investigate common footballing hypotheses:

* **Creativity vs. Retaining Possession:** Plot `Touches` vs `Dispossessed`. Do players who see the ball more often turn it over more, or are some exceptionally secure under pressure?
* **Dribbling Volume vs. Threat:** Plot `Carries` against `Carries Ended with Chance` to see which wingers or midfielders carry the ball effectively versus those who run into dead ends.
* **Shot Volume vs. Quality:** Plot `Shots` against `Shots On Target` to evaluate shooting accuracy across teams or positions.

### 5. Club & Position-Level Group Aggregations

* **Team Playing Styles:** Group by `Club` and aggregate metrics to define how teams play. For example, sum up `Crosses` vs `Through Balls` to see if a team prefers attacking down the wings or through the center. Or aggregate `Possession Won` to identify the league's high-pressing teams.
* **Positional Profiles:** Aggregate metrics by `Position` (`DEF`, `MID`, `FWD`, `GKP`) to map out standard baselines for what an average Premier League defender vs midfielder achieves.

### 6. Advanced Machine Learning (Clustering & Classification)

* **Tactical Role Clustering (K-Means):** Instead of standard positions like "MID," you can use variables like `Tackles`, `Through Balls`, `Touches`, and `Goals` to cluster players into modern tactical roles (e.g., *Deep-Lying Playmakers, Box-to-Box Midfielders, Wing-backs, Target Men, Pressing Forwards*).
* **Player Similarity Finder (Recommendation Engine):** Write an algorithm where you choose a player (e.g., *Bukayo Saka*) and find the top 5 closest players in the league based on Euclidean distance or Cosine similarity across relevant performance metrics. This mimics real-world scouting!
